# LuxeCart — DTC Fashion E-Commerce Analysis (2022–2024)

**35,000 Orders | 8,000 Customers | 4 Markets | Full Commercial Diagnostic**

---

## Overview

A complete end-to-end commercial diagnostic of LuxeCart — a direct-to-consumer fashion accessories brand selling Bags, Jewellery, Watches, Sunglasses, Scarves, and Belts across the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia.

This analysis covers revenue performance, product and category profitability, customer acquisition and loyalty, seasonal demand patterns, discount strategy impact, returns diagnostics, and payment method optimization — closing with eight prioritized strategic recommendations.

---

## Key Findings

- **$7,067,314 total revenue** across 35,000 orders over 34 months
- **94.6% repeat purchase rate** — world-class loyalty that is generating zero structured incremental revenue
- **Bags return at 12%** — 3x the brand average — driven by product information gaps, not product quality
- **Paid Social drives highest volume but lowest AOV** — a classic paid acquisition trap causing budget misallocation
- **BNPL customers generate the highest AOV** of any payment method — but BNPL is buried at checkout
- **34.8% of orders carry a discount** — risking customer conditioning to wait for promotions
- **Tuesday–Thursday outperform weekends** — counter-intuitive pattern with direct campaign timing implications
- **35–44 age group has highest AOV** but receives no targeted marketing treatment
